CompTIA A+

Issued Jul 2019 · Expires Jul 2028

The CompTIA A+ certification is a widely recognized credential for entry-level IT professionals. It validates foundational skills in areas such as hardware, software, networking, and troubleshooting.

AWS Certified Cloud practitioner

Issued Jan 2026 · Expires Jan 2029

The AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner certification is an entry-level credential that validates a candidate's understanding of the AWS Cloud, its services, and basic architectural principles. It is designed for individuals who are new to cloud computing and want to demonstrate their knowledge of AWS.
